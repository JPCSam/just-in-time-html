Specialist Diagnostics

Is your fault light on? Are you experiencing computer problems in your vehicle? If so, do not ignore it. It might be more serious than it seems. Come and see us for a diagnostic check and we’ll keep your car running as it should.

Don’t you just plug in a computer?

Yes, it’s true that we plug in a computer to help with the diagnostic evaluation. But there’s a lot more to it than that. You see, a single fault code can have many different causes, so we follow a set process that guarantees we discover the root cause of the issue the first time around. And that keeps the diagnosis efficient and cost-effective.

Help! I’ve got a fault light on!

We can definitely help if you’ve got a fault light on. A diagnostic service is the perfect service for finding out why your fault light is on. Bring your car to our Tadley service centre and we’ll find out why the light’s on.
