I can't find a bulb in my car lights?

That’s probably because your vehicle uses LEDs. Although LEDs are a great advancement in car lighting technology, changing them can be tricky. If you think your car may have LEDs, come and see us. One of our technicians can change them for you.

Can I change my bulbs myself?

If you have the right equipment and the technical know-how you can change your own bulbs. Be warned though, we often end up repairing damaged car lights after people have tried to change their own bulbs and made things worse. So instead of paying for a cheap, quick fix, they have to fork out for much more costly repairs.
