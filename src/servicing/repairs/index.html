Car Repairs

If your car needs repairing, don’t put it off. Even small faults can be dangerous, illegal and even lead to something much more serious if left for too long.

If you aren’t sure what the problem is, don’t fret, our expert technicians can get to the bottom of it. We also have diagnostic equipment that helps us identify any electrical faults within the engine.

Electric Vehicle Repairs

Electric vehicles can have different wear rates for items such as brakes and tyres than regular combustion engine vehicles, so it’s important to have regular checks on these items by our trained technician. They also have different maintenance requirements (no engine oil change), but may have additional filters or cooling systems that need attention.

We offer a wide range of car repair services, including:

  • Brakes
  • Suspension
  • Engine diagnostics and repairs
  • Electrical system repairs
