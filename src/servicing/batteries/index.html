Why is my battery so important?

Your battery is an extremely vital part of your car’s engine and with the growth of in-car technology such as air conditioning, digital music players and satellite navigation systems it is important to make sure your battery maintains its optimum working level. All of these factors place a greater strain on your car's battery as well as increase the demand on your car's charging system which can lead to premature battery failure. Regular battery checks can help avoid these failures and breakdowns!

Here are a few common signs that your car’s battery may need replacing:

  • Difficulty starting the engine or it not starting first time
  • Electrical malfunctions such as lights or dashboard lights not working properly
  • Poor performance in cold weather

If you are experiencing any of the above we strongly suggest you come and see us, whatever car you’ve got we have the right battery for you.
