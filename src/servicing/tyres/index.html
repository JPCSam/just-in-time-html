TYRES

Tyre Repair

It is essential that your tyres are maintained to an impeccable standard, not only because it is a legal requirement but because they are the point of contact between your car and the road.

The minimum depth allowed on your tyres is 1.6 millimeters and this is measured across the central ¾ of the tread which is found around the tyre’s complete circumference. It is advised that your tyres are replaced before you reach this level of depth, we recommend that your tyres are replaced once they have reached a depth of 3 millimeters. A deeper tread gives a stronger braking power which is a necessity, especially in challenging driving conditions and weather.

We are more than happy to help if you are unsure of what level your tyres are at. We can then provide replacement tyres if needed to keep you safe while also complying with road traffic regulations.

Wheel Alignment

One of our main concerns is keeping our community safe on the road. And wheel alignment is a vital part of vehicle safety.
Our technicians have been delivering high-quality wheel alignment for years. Using the best equipment for the job, we work with precision to ensure your wheels are perfectly aligned and offer premium performance.

“Does wheel alignment really matter?"

Wheel alignment matters more than you think yet it’s overlooked by many drivers. How often do you drive over speed bumps and potholes on the road? One might not be so bad but over time they have a major impact on the alignment of your vehicle. Not only does good wheel alignment keep you safe, but it also reduces the wear of your tyres and fuel consumption. So keeping your wheels aligned keeps money in your pocket too!

“How do I know if my alignment is wrong?"

It’s important to be aware of how your car is performing with regards to your wheel alignment. If you’re experiencing any of the following, give us a call.

  • Vehicle pulling to the left or right
  • Uneven or rapid tyre wear
  • Your steering wheel is crooked when driving straight
  • Squealing tyres
  • Shaking or vibrations at high speeds
