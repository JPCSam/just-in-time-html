Clutches

Specialist Clutch Servicing & Repair

Driving with a damaged gearbox or clutch can be dangerous, not to mention frustrating. If you think there may be a problem with your clutch or gearbox, get it checked as soon as you can. Although the parts of your vehicle inevitably get worn down over time, clutch and gearbox issues can have other causes too.

Our skilled technicians have the knowledge needed to diagnose and fix your problem, whether you’re on a manual, semi-automatic or automatic gearbox. We only use the most advanced equipment and premium parts to do the job. That’s a promise. So if you’re worried about the condition of your clutch or gearbox, get yourself to our Bracknell service centre.
