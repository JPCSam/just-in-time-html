My catalyst has failed, can you help?

Located between the engine and the muffler, the catalyst is one of the final pieces of defence against air pollution from your vehicle. Catalytic converters normally last for 10 years or more, but they can become contaminated, clogged, overheated or physically damaged, resulting in sluggish engine performance or even engine shutdown. Often, a catalytic converter failure is a symptom of a problem somewhere else in the vehicle. And you must get that problem fixed or the replacement catalyst will be destroyed. Bring your vehicle into ECO Test and let us fix it for you.

My car’s failed the emissions test

Can you help if my car has failed the emissions test? It comes as a surprise to most drivers when they fail emissions because their vehicle seems to be functioning fine. The bad news is you cannot drive without passing the emissions test. So time is of the essence when it comes to getting those repairs done. Your exhaust system is often the cause of your emissions failure. Bring your vehicle into our Bracknell garage and we’ll soon have you sorted and back on the road.
