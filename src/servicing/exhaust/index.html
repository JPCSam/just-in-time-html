How can I tell if there is a problem with my exhaust?

  • Rattling noise coming from underneath your car
  • Hissing noises
  • Strong smell of fumes

Catalytic Converters

Your catalytic converter helps to reduce airborne pollutants that could harm people as well as the environment, turning 90% of harmful emissions into less harmful gasses. A well-maintained catalytic converter improves the efficiency of your car as well as keeping you safe from harmful gasses such as hydrocarbons, carbon monoxide and nitrogen oxides.

Why is it important to regularly check on my exhaust?

A well maintained exhaust keeps your car quiet and helps the engine run properly. It also maintains fuel efficiency, keeps fumes out of the car and reduces your emissions. Keeping your emissions down is also a legal requirement as your car will not pass its MOT if they are too high, this is another important reason to make sure you check your exhaust regularly.
