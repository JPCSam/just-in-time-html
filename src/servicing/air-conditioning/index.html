Air Conditioning

Specialist Air-Conditioning Servicing & Repair

Most people don’t realise your car’s air conditioning plays a major role in your vehicle’s heating and ventilation system. So you need to make sure your air-con stays in good condition all year round. Even in those cold winter months when the last thing you want is cooler air in your car, you still need to look after your air conditioning.

Why? Because your air-con doesn’t just cool air, it dehumidifies it, which is great for demisting your windscreen. Having your air conditioning system serviced every 24 months can save you hundreds of pounds in pricey repairs. So don’t leave it too long and maybe discuss it with our technicians while having a service.
