SERVICING

Do I need a service?

Just in Time are pleased to provide car servicing solutions for all motorists local to Oxfordshire, Berkshire and Hampshire.
Regular servicing is an integral part of making sure your whole vehicle is working as it should - and performing to its best. When your car operates efficiently, your engine runs a lot smoother, meaning you use less fuel. Simple and necessary tasks like changing the oil and ensuring your tyres are properly inflated improve the performance of your car and help you to lower the cost of running your vehicle. We are thrilled to say we undertake services for all leading car manufacturers, as well as both combustion and electric vehicles.

“I've just had an MOT, why do I need to have my car serviced too?"

A lot of drivers think that passing an MOT each year is enough to take a preventative approach and catch mechanical faults that may occur. But servicing is actually completely different. An MOT is an annual inspection for safety - it's not designed to replace or repair worn vehicle components. Regular servicing can lead to an early diagnosis which ensures problems are resolved quickly and most likely for a lower cost than if a minor issue evolved into a fundamental problem.
A serviced car is a reliable car!

"What's involved in a full service?"

  • An engine oil change and/or filter replacement
  • Checking lights, tyres, exhaust and operations of brakes and steering
  • Checking hydraulic fluid and coolant levels
  • Checking the cooling system (from radiators in your car to pumps and hoses)
  • Suspension checks
  • Steering alignment
  • Testing the car’s battery condition
