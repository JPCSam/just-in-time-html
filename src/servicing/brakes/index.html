Why do my brakes need servicing?

Brakes are a crucial component of your vehicle and it is important that they are maintained to a high standard for your safety. Your brakes must meet minimum specifications to be classed as safe. If you are concerned about the performance of your brakes and you would like them to be checked by one of our skilled mechanics, get in touch with us today. They will be able to check whether your bakes are safe or if any components need to be replaced.

When should I be concerned about the performance of my brakes?

  • Grinding
  • Squeaking
  • Pulling
  • Pulsating
  • Sponginess or a soft brake pedal
