DPF

Specialist DPF Servicing & Repair

Unless you’re a car fanatic or one of our technicians, you probably won’t know what a DPF is. It’s one of the least known components of a car. But it’s mighty important. Your Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) traps and stores soot. That’s its job.

DPFs were imposed by EU regulations to pass emissions tests. If yours is faulty and you don’t get it fixed you could face a 4 figure fine. Your DPF is designed to burn off the accumulated soot. However, they can get blocked from time to time.

What are the common causes of the DPF light coming on?

A failed exhaust gas recirculation valve, issues with your exhaust and injection systems and faulty glow plugs are all common causes of your DPF warning light coming on. Even using the wrong type of oil in a car can trigger the warning light.

Come and see us at ECO Test. One of our technicians will quickly identify the cause and get your DPF working as it should.

How do I stop my DPF blocking again?

The best way to make sure your DPF doesn’t clog up again is by bringing your car to ECO Test and letting one of our technicians regenerate your system by burning all the soot from your filter
