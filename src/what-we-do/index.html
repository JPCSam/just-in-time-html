WHAT WE DO

Automotive

Servicing & Repair

We cover a wide range of automotive servicing and maintenance jobs such as; general servicing, clutches, cambelts, brakes, suspension and even welding. There's no job too big or too small.

Diagnostics

Does your car have an issue you can’t quite get to the bottom of? Feel free to get in touch to discuss, we are available for callouts to diagnose and repair issues wherever you are!

Modification

If you're feeling like adding your own personal touch to your car, we are also able to carry out modifications such as suspension, exhausts and much more!

We also provide...

  • Tyre fitting and wheel balancing

  • Vehicle recovery & transportation

  • Supply & fit printed number plates

  • Air conditioning re-gassing & diagnostics

Marine

Service & Repair

We are available to carry out a wide range of marine servicing and repairs, if it’s broken - we can fix it.

Diagnostics

If you are having some issues with your boat, but can't quite put your finger on the problem, we're able to diagnose your issue.

Restoration

Has your boat been off the water for a few years and in need of some love? Get in touch and we can get it water worthy again!
